Classic WoW Stat Conversion Mechanics

Complete guide to stat conversions and scaling in World of Warcraft Classic

💪 Strength Conversions

Melee Attack Power
  • Warrior/Paladin/Shaman/Druid: 2 AP per 1 Strength
  • Other classes: 1 AP per 1 Strength
Block Value
  • 1 Block Damage per 20 Strength points

⚡ Agility Conversions

Armor Rating
  • 2 Armor per 1 Agility (all classes)
Critical Strike Chance
  • Hunter: 1% per 53 Agility
  • Rogue: 1% per 29 Agility
  • Paladin/Shaman/Warrior/Druid: 1% per 20 Agility
Dodge Chance
  • Rogue: 1% per 14.5 Agility
  • Hunter: 1% per 26 Agility
Attack Power
  • Rogue/Druid/Hunter: 1 Melee AP per 1 Agility
  • Rogue/Warrior: 1 Ranged AP per 1 Agility
  • Hunter: 2 Ranged AP per 1 Agility

🧠 Intellect Conversions

Mana Pool
  • 15 Mana per 1 Intellect (all classes)
Spell Critical Strike
  • Varies by class: 54-60.6 Intellect per 1% crit

❤️ Stamina Conversions

Health Points
  • 10 Health per 1 Stamina (all classes)

✨ Spirit Conversions

Mana Regeneration
  • Varies by class: 12.5-17 Spirit per 5 MP5

Master Your Character Stats!

Understanding these conversion rates is crucial for optimizing your character's performance in Classic WoW. Use this information to make informed decisions about gear upgrades and stat priorities.

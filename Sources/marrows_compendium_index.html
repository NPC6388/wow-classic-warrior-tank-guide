Marrow's Compendium of Dragonslaying

A comprehensive Fury Warrior guide for World of Warcraft Classic
This guide aims to help the reader from the ground up: from the foundational mechanics of World of Warcraft combat, to an in-depth overview of warrior abilities and their utilization.

Contents Overview

Combat Mechanics Covered

  • Miss Chance - Understanding hit rating and weapon skill interactions
  • Glancing Blows - Damage reduction mechanics against higher level targets
  • Weapon Skill - Critical breakpoints and their impact on DPS
  • Crit Cap - Attack table limitations and optimization
  • Flurry - Talent mechanics and uptime calculations

Guide Philosophy

  • Guide described as "a work in progress"
  • Reflects best understanding of warrior mechanics as of publication date
  • Information may evolve as the meta develops
  • Emphasizes player choice and enjoyment over rigid optimization
  • Aims to help players "push the envelope of their class"

What This Guide Does NOT Cover

  • DPS simulations (recommends external tools)
  • Specific gear recommendations (contextual nature of gearing)
  • Encounter-specific strategies

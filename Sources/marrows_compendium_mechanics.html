Combat Mechanics

Melee Attack Types

World of Warcraft combat distinguishes between two primary attack types:

  • White (auto) attacks - Your character's automatic weapon swings
  • Yellow (special) attacks - Abilities like Bloodthirst, Execute, and Heroic Strike
Key Difference: Yellow attacks cannot glance, making them more reliable for damage output against higher level targets.

Miss Chance Mechanics

  • Base 8% miss chance against mobs 3 levels higher (level 63 bosses)
  • Miss probability depends on weapon skill and target level difference
  • Dual wielding increases miss probability significantly
  • Weapon skill of 305 reduces miss chance to 6% for special attacks

Glancing Blow Mechanics

Critical Information: Glancing blows are one of the most significant DPS losses for melee classes in Classic WoW.
  • Occurs only against equal or higher level enemies
  • 40% chance to glance against level 63 enemies with 300 weapon skill
  • Glancing blows deal reduced damage (as low as 65% of maximum damage)
  • Cannot critically hit
  • Affects only white damage (auto attacks)

Weapon Skill Impact

Weapon skill is arguably the most important stat for melee DPS classes:

Benefits of Increased Weapon Skill

  • Reduces miss chance - Each point above 300 = 0.04% less miss
  • Reduces glancing blow damage penalty - Higher skill = less damage reduction
  • Reduces target dodge chance - Marginal but measurable improvement
  • Reduces target parry chance - Prevents parry-haste acceleration

Optimal Weapon Skill Breakpoints

  • 305 weapon skill - Critical breakpoint for raid bosses
  • 308+ weapon skill - Diminishing returns, glancing damage cap reached
  • Racial bonuses - Human/Orc/Troll racial weapon skills are extremely valuable

Critical Strike Cap

The critical strike cap is calculated by subtracting all avoidance mechanisms:

Crit Cap = 100% - Miss% - Dodge% - Block% - Parry% - Glancing%

  • Increases with better gear and world buffs
  • Varies significantly based on weapon skill and hit rating
  • Different for white attacks vs yellow attacks

Flurry Mechanics

Flurry Talent: 5-point talent increasing attack speed by 30% for 3 swings after a critical hit.
  • Critical strike chance does not directly translate to 100% uptime
  • Multiple crits while Flurry is active do not stack or refresh duration
  • Diminishing returns on critical strike chance for flurry uptime
  • Extremely valuable for sustained DPS increase

Hit Rating Mechanics Against Level 63 Raid Bosses

Understanding hit mechanics is crucial for DPS optimization in Classic WoW. Against level 63 raid bosses, the following avoidance chances apply:

  • 8% miss chance for special "yellow" attacks
  • 8% miss chance for melee auto attacks (non-dual wielding)
  • 26.4% miss chance for dual-wielding melee auto attacks
  • 6.5% dodge chance
  • 14% parry chance (when attacking from front)
  • 5% block chance (when attacking from front)
  • 16% miss chance for spells

Key Hit Rating Calculations

  • The first 1% hit is ignored if boss defense is more than 10 points above your weapon skill
  • Weapon skill directly impacts both miss and dodge chances
  • The commonly cited 9% "hit cap" is not a hard cap - it's the effective soft cap

Weapon Skill Impact Formula

Miss Chance Calculation:

skillDifference = Target Defense - Your Weapon Skill

if (skillDifference > 10) {
    missChance = 5 + (skillDifference * 0.2)
} else {
    missChance = 5 + (skillDifference * 0.1)
}

  • Dodge Chance: 6.0%

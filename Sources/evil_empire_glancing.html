Glancing Blow Mechanics

Evil Empire's glancing blow guide likely covered:

  • The formula: Glancing blow chance increases with target level
  • Damage reduction: Typically 35% reduction for glancing blows
  • Only affects white damage (auto-attacks), not special abilities
  • Cannot be avoided - always part of the attack table vs higher level targets
  • Weapon skill reduces glancing blow chance and damage reduction
  • Critical importance for raid DPS calculations

Historical Significance

Understanding glancing blows was crucial for Classic WoW players, as they represented a significant DPS loss when fighting raid bosses. Evil Empire's mathematical approach would have provided precise calculations for optimization.

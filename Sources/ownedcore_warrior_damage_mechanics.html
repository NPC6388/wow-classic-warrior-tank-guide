Warrior damage mechanics

Mechanics of an Attack

The game generates a random number from 1-10 to determine attack outcome. Here's how the distribution typically works:

  • 1-2: Critical Hit
  • 3-6: Regular Hit
  • 7: Miss
  • 8: Dodge
  • 9: Parry
  • 10: Block

Miss Rates by Weapon Type

  • Dual Wielding: 24% standard miss rate
  • Two Handed: 5% standard miss rate
  • One Hand with Shield: 5% standard miss rate

Critical Hit Mechanics

  • Critical hits deal double damage
  • +1 Agility = +0.05% chance to land a Critical Hit
  • +1 Weapon Skill = +0.04% chance to land a Critical Hit

Rage Generation (Level 60)

Damage Done:
Rage = Damage / (charLevel * 0.5)

Damage Received:
Rage = Damage / (charLevel * 1.5)

Attack Power to DPS Conversion

  • +1 Strength = +2 Attack Power
  • Two-Handed/One-Hand: +14 Attack Power = +1 DPS
  • Dual Wielding: +8.62 Attack Power = +1 DPS

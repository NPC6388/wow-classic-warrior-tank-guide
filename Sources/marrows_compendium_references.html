Research Timeline & Context

Source Period: 2006-2020

This research spans from the original World of Warcraft launch through the Classic re-release, incorporating:

  • Vanilla Era (2006-2007): Original theorycrafting and blue post clarifications
  • Private Server Era (2007-2019): Community research and simulation development
  • Classic Era (2019-2020): Verification and refinement of mechanics understanding

All sources focus specifically on World of Warcraft Classic warrior mechanics and optimization strategies, ensuring relevance to the Classic game environment.

Tankenheimer macros

General:

Generic macro format
#showtooltip <name of ability> 
/cast <name of stance>
/cast <name of ability>
/startattack


I bind this to mousewheel down
#showtooltip heroic strike
/startattack
/cast !Heroic Strike


Tanking:

Taunt
#showtooltip Taunt
/startattack [harm,nodead]
/cast Defensive Stance
/cast [@mouseover,harm,nodead][] Taunt
/ra Taunted %T
/p Taunted %T

LIP + Shout (needs 2 GCDs)
#showtooltip Challenging Shout
/stopcasting
/equipslot 16 <MH weapon name>
/equipslot 17 <shield name>
/startattack
/p AOE TAUNT
/RAID AOE TAUNT
/use Limited Invulnerability Potion
/cast Challenging Shout

Flask set PRE-PULL (requires ItemRack or similar)(slot 14 is the bottom trinket slot)(I have one of these for each of my fight-specific sets)
#showtooltip diamond flask
/itemrack equip <name of flask set>
/click pause 0.5
/use 14
/click pause 0.5
/itemrack equip <Name of tanking set>

Remove bubble (add to any macro)
/cancelaura power word: shield

If you need to execute while tanking (eg. Vael) (I bind this to mousewheel up)
#showtooltip Execute
/startattack
/stopcasting
/cast Battle Stance
/cast Execute
/cast Heroic Strike

Mocking Blow
#showtooltip mocking blow
/startattack
/cast battle stance
/cast mocking blow

Equip shield, eat Stoneshield, cast Shield Block (needs 2 GCDs)
#showtooltip Shield block
/startattack
/stopcasting [noworn:Shields]
/equipslot 17 <name of shield>
/equipslot 16 <MH weapon name>
/cast Defensive Stance
/use Greater Stoneshield Potion
/cast shield block

Shield Wall
#showtooltip Shield Wall
/startattack [combat,harm,nodead]
/stopcasting [noworn:Shields]
/equipslot 17 <name of shield>
/equipslot 16 <MH weapon name>
/cast defensive stance
/cast Shield Wall
/ra Shield Wall activated

Last Stand
#showtooltip last stand
/startattack [combat,harm,nodead]
/stopcasting [noworn:Shields]
/equipslot 17 <name of shield>
/equipslot 16 <MH weapon name>
/cast defensive stance
/cast last stand
/ra Last Stand activated

DW to Shield swap toggle
/equipslot 16 <MH weapon name>
/equipslot 17 <OH weapon name>
/stopcasting
/equipslot 16 <MH weapon name>
/equipslot 17 <shield name>

Nef p3 adds (spam it)
#showtooltip Challenging Shout
/p AOE TAUNT
/RAID AOE TAUNT
 /use Limited Invulnerability Potion
 /cast Challenging Shout
 /use Goblin Sapper Charge

/cast berserker rage

Apply stone to OH with Rightclick, MH with Leftclick (OH-only for Horde)
#showtooltip Elemental Sharpening Stone
/use [button:1] Elemental Sharpening Stone; Elemental Sharpening Stone
/use [button:1] 16; 17
/click StaticPopup1Button1

Spam this to equip both daggers for Viscidus
/castsequence reset=2 Coldrage Dagger 
/equipslot 17 Coldrage Dagger

One button to use whatever ranged you have equipped (you still have to equip the right ammo)
#showtooltip
/cast [worn:Bows] Shoot Bow; [worn:Crossbows] Shoot Crossbow; [worn:Guns] Shoot Gun; [worn:Thrown] Throw

DPS Stat Priority (PvE)

The following stat priority assumes you have achieved 300 weapon skill and are optimizing for raid DPS performance.

Priority Order:

  1. 9% Hit Rating - Essential for consistent DPS output
  2. Strength and Attack Power - Primary damage scaling stats
  3. Agility and Crit Chance - Secondary damage amplification
Key Point: Hit rating takes absolute priority until you reach the 9% cap for special abilities. Missing attacks is a complete DPS loss that cannot be compensated by other stats.

Stat Conversion Mechanics

Stat Conversion Rate Effect
Strength 1 STR = 2 AP Increases melee damage and blocking value
Agility 20 AGI = 1% Crit Increases crit chance, dodge, and armor
Stamina 1 STA = 10 HP Increases health pool for survivability
Hit Rating Varies by level Reduces miss chance against targets

Weapon Skill Benefits

Weapon skill above 300 provides significant DPS improvements against raid bosses (level 63):

  • Reduces miss chance - Each point decreases miss chance by 0.04%
  • Decreases glancing blow penalty - Reduces damage loss from glancing blows
  • Increases critical hit chance - Each point adds 0.04% crit against higher level targets
  • Improves white damage consistency - More reliable auto-attack damage

Hit Rating Caps by Content

Required Hit Percentages:

  • PvP (same level): 5% Hit Rating
  • PvE Special Abilities (behind boss): 9% Hit Rating
  • PvE Auto Attacks (behind boss): 26.4% Hit Rating
  • Dual Wield Penalty: +24% additional miss chance

Note: The 26.4% auto-attack cap is generally not practical to reach, so focus on the 9% special ability cap.

Critical Strike Mechanics

  • Damage Multiplier: Physical crits deal 200% damage (100% bonus)
  • Attack Table Interaction: Crit chance is reduced by miss and dodge chances
  • Positioning Impact: Standing behind targets eliminates dodge from the attack table
  • Flurry Synergy: Critical hits trigger Flurry for increased attack speed
Crit Cap Consideration: Your effective crit cap varies based on your hit rating and positioning. Higher hit rating allows for more effective crit chance by reducing misses.

20 and 40-Man Boss Encounters

Zul'Gurub (20-man)

Jeklik: MT - Tanks boss with positioning for fire bombs, OT - Uses challenging shout for bat waves
Venoxis: MT - Main tank, OT - Handles snake adds
Mar'li: MT - Tanks boss, OT - Controls spider adds
Bloodlord Mandokir: MT - Main tank, OT - Taunts after charge/threat reset
Jin'do: MT - Tanks boss, OT - Controls skeleton adds
Gahz'ranka: MT - Main tank, OT - Backup positioning
Thekal: MT - P1 tanks Thekal, OT - P1 tanks zealots, P2 backup for tiger form
Arlokk: MT - Tanks boss, OT - Handles panther adds
Hakkar: MT/OT - Tank swap due to mind control

Ahn'Qiraj 20 (20-man)

Kurinnaxx: MT/OT - Tank swap for mortal wound stacks, avoid sand traps
General Rajaxx: MT - Tanks boss after NPCs die, OT - Controls wave captains
Moam: MT - Main tank with arcane protection, OT - Backup tank
Buru: Kiting encounter (no traditional tanking), all tanks control broodling adds
Ayamiss: MT - Ground phase tank, OT - Backup tank
Ossirian: MT - Main tank, OT - Backup tank

Molten Core (40-man)

Lucifron: MT - Tanks boss, OT - Pulls protector adds away from raid (kill adds first)
Magmadar: MT - Main tank with tremor totem/fear ward, OT - Don't tank on rune
Gehennas: MT - Tanks boss center, OT - Stack adds behind pillar (curse priority on tanks)
Garr: MT - Taunts boss from add tank, OT - Flask set for threat, Force Reactive Disk + oil of immolation
Shazzrah: MT - Boss is tauntable, recklessness opener, OT - GAPP prepop, all CDs at start
Baron Geddon: MT - 200+ FR buffed, Last Stand before inferno, living bomb left side positioning
Golemagg: MT - Stack everything, OT - Stack adds on boss for cleave (DPS focus boss)
Sulfuron Harbinger: MT - Tank boss away from adds, OT - Stack flame spawns (interrupt rotation)
Domo: MT - Tank across pit after teleport, OT - Stack adds (elites → healers → elites)
Ragnaros: MT/OT - GFPP prepop, taunt after wrath, taunt swap last 30%, sons during submerge

Blackwing Lair (40-man)

Razorgore: MT - P1 tank adds, orb last 3 eggs, OT - P1 tank adds (odd north/even south), P2 face boss away
Vaelastrasz: MT - Max threat pre-pull setup, east position, OT - Backup tank
Broodlord Lashlayer: MT/OT - Flask set pull, Greater Stoneshield always, shield opener, swap after mortal strike/knock away
Firemaw: MT - Flask set pull, main tank, OT - Stay out, taunt for wing buffet, run back out
Ebonroc: MT - Main tank, OT - Taunt sets, eat wing buffets
Flamegor: MT - Main tank, OT - Taunt sets, eat shadow flame
Chromaggus: MT - Main tank, OT - Backup tank
Nefarian: MT/OT - P1 tank adds on square, position for Nef at 3 adds left, P3 any war/bear LIP+AoE taunt adds

Ahn'Qiraj 40 (40-man)

The Prophet Skeram: MT - GAPP prepop, focus unmarked copy on splits, OT - Controls images
Bug Trio: MT/OT - LIP ready for Yauj brood, spread adds, keep boss from DPS
Battleguard Sartura: MT/OT - Taunt rotation callouts, LIP ready, spread adds from DPS
Fankriss: MT - Enter first, DPS from behind, OT - AoE worm adds at spawn
Viscidus: MT - GNPP prepop, frost weapons required, OT - Shatter phase add control
Princess Huhuran: MT - GNPP, poison cleanse only tanks, OT - Stack underneath at 35%
Twin Emperors: MT - Vek'nilash (unbalancing strike swaps), OT - Vek'lor (warlock tank)
Ouro: MT - Emergence tank, OT - Submerge positioning
C'Thun: MT - 8 group positioning, tentacle control, OT - Stomach coordination

Naxxramas (40-man)

Anub'Rekhan: MT - Tank adds on boss, east side locust swarm, OT - Melee edge positioning
Grand Widow Faerlina: MT - Tank on top of followers, OT - Worshipper left side sacrifice rotation
Maexxna: MT - Web spray preparation, OT - AoE taunt spiderling rotation
Noth: MT - Stack middle, 2s DPS pause after blink, OT - Guardian spawn positioning
Heigan: MT - P1 platform, P2 dance, OT - Eye stalk tunnel (6 people, right hug)
Loatheb: MT - Full HoTs pre-pull, spore positioning, OT - NW grate healer coordination
Instructor Razuvious: MT - Mind control understudy (shield wall/taunt), OT - LoS stairs backup
Gothik: MT - Live side 3 bay positioning, OT - Dead side tank adds away from healers
Four Horsemen: 8 tanks - skull→cross→square→moon front, triangle→diamond→circle→star back
Patchwerk: MT - Steady damage, 3 OT - Full mitigation, Greater Stoneshield, no Shield Block
Grobbulus: MT - Always behind boss, OT - Tank slimes on boss but behind
Gluth: MT - Tunnel entry, fear LoS left pillar, OT - Build threat, dodge 5th+ fear
Thaddius: MT/OT - Platform positioning (melee right/casters left), main platform polarity
Sapphiron: MT - Front/rear leg positioning, OT - Air phase group coordination
Kel'Thuzad: MT - P1 abom pickup, P2 stack tight save rage, OT - P3 guardian pickup/shackle
Tank Assignment Strategy: These roles can vary significantly based on raid composition, strategy, and gear level. Communicate with your raid leader about specific tank assignments and always be prepared to adapt.
Complex Encounter Notes:
• Four Horsemen: Requires precise mark rotation - tanks move between corners every 3 marks, coordinating with healers and DPS rotations
• Gothik: Live side manages waves (trainee→rider→DK), Dead side handles spectral versions - requires tight coordination between both sides
• Gluth: Involves zombie kiting with druid + warrior slowing, mage frost nova rotation, and decimate phase burst AoE
• C'Thun: 8-group positioning system for tentacle management, stomach phase requires dedicated add control and acid stack awareness

Encounter-Specific Requirements

Resistance Requirements

Fire Resistance Fights

Molten Core:
• Ragnaros: 315 Fire Resistance (uncapped) for tank survivability
• Baron Geddon: 200+ Fire Resistance minimum, Living Bomb immunity at 315
• Golemagg: 150+ Fire Resistance for DoT management

Blackwing Lair:
• Vaelastrasz: 150+ Fire Resistance minimum for Burning Adrenaline
• Flamegor: 200+ Fire Resistance for Shadow Flame breath

AQ40:
• Twin Emperors: 300+ Fire Resistance for Vek'lor tanking

Nature Resistance Fights

AQ40:
• Princess Huhuran: 200+ Nature Resistance for Poison Bolt Volley
• Viscidus: 200+ Nature Resistance helpful for positioning

AQ20:
• Ayamiss: 150+ Nature Resistance for ranged damage

Frost Resistance Fights

Naxxramas:
• Sapphiron: 200+ Frost Resistance minimum, 315 optimal
• Kel'Thuzad: 100+ Frost Resistance for Frost Bolt damage

Shadow Resistance Fights

Naxxramas:
• Loatheb: 150+ Shadow Resistance for Necrotic Aura
• Various encounters with shadow damage (Maexxna, etc.)

Stamina & Survivability Requirements

High HP Encounters

• Patchwerk (Naxx): 8,500+ HP minimum for consistent hateful strike survival
• Four Horsemen (Naxx): 9,000+ HP optimal for mark stacking mechanics
• Twin Emperors (AQ40): 8,000+ HP for teleport damage and tank swaps
• C'Thun (AQ40): 7,500+ HP for eye beam and tentacle damage
• Kel'Thuzad (Naxx): 8,000+ HP for frost bolt and frostbolt volley

Defense & Avoidance Requirements

Critical Hit Immunity Priority:
• All Naxxramas Encounters: 440+ defense (or Shield Block uptime) essential
• AQ40 Bosses: 440+ defense recommended for consistent tanking
• BWL Encounters: 415+ defense minimum, 440+ preferred

Threat & Hit Requirements

High Threat Encounters:
• Vaelastrasz (BWL): Maximum threat stats due to infinite rage
• Twin Emperors (AQ40): High hit/expertise for reliable tank swaps
• Four Horsemen (Naxx): 9% hit minimum for mark rotation reliability
• Multi-add Encounters: Hit cap essential for threat pickup (Razorgore, Gothik)

Special Mechanical Requirements

Positioning & Movement Intensive:
• Razorgore (BWL): High mobility, instant threat generation for add control
• Nefarian (BWL): Class call preparation (weapon swapping for disarm call)
• Thaddius (Naxx): Polarity management with threat maintenance
• Sapphiron (Naxx): Air phase positioning with maximum survivability focus
Final Note: This guide represents current meta and theorycrafting understanding as of 2025. Always validate optimization results against established simulators and real-world testing. Strategies may vary based on raid composition, gear availability, and guild preferences.
